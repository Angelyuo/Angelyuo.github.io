About EduGames

EduGames is a company that revolutionizes education by fusing gaming with education. Offering distinctive educational options that involve and entertain students of all ages and backgrounds is at the forefront of the company's vision. The creation of distinctive learning opportunities focused on equity and accessibility in the digital age are two of EduGames' primary offerings, with Minecraft Education Edition and Duolingo being two of their first services to classrooms.

In order to incorporate their solutions into both official and informal learning, EduGames collaborates with educators and educational institutions. By leveraging the widespread appeal of gaming to provide captivating online learning opportunities, EduGames is transforming learning as a whole.
