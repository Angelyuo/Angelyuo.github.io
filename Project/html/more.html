Who are we?

We are EduGames, a startup that uses gaming to transform education, offering unique learning experiences for all ages and backgrounds. Our vision is to provide fairness and accessibility in the digital age, with Minecraft Education Edition and Duolingo being their first classroom services. We collaborate with educators and institutions to capitalize on gaming's widespread appeal to provide entertaining online learning experiences.


What are we about?

Our company is about changing the face of education by merging gaming and learning to meet the requirements and preferences of today's students. We are using gaming to make learning more engaging, accessible, and inclusive. We promote inclusion and equity by making education accessible to all via the use of digital platforms and technology. We also emphasize 21st-century skill development, incorporating it into its educational offerings via videogame mechanics, collaborative effort, and real-world puzzle solving.
